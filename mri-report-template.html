What makes Pisum MRI templates different.

Sequence-aware structure

Each MRI template includes fields for sequences performed (T1, T2, FLAIR, DWI, ADC, post-contrast T1), field strength, and contrast agent — pre-organized, never forgotten.

Protocol-specific scoring systems

PI-RADS for prostate, LI-RADS for liver, BI-RADS for breast MRI — scoring fields are integrated directly into the relevant templates.

Sally AI understands MRI-specific vocabulary across all templates. Dictate "T2 hyperintense lesion in the right posterior temporal lobe" — it goes to the right field, in the right language.
