AI that works for radiology

Why AI makes radiology reporting better — and why offline matters.

AI is fundamentally changing radiology workflow. The most impactful application isn't image analysis — it's report generation. Radiologists spend up to 40% of their shift on written documentation: boilerplate phrases, repetitive section structure, copy-paste from template to template.

An AI radiology report generator eliminates that friction. Instead of dictating into a blank document and manually organizing content, the AI routes findings to the correct report section, applies consistent medical terminology, and produces a formatted output ready for export.

The remaining question is: where does the AI run? Cloud-based AI report generators process your dictation on remote servers — meaning patient voice data leaves your institution. For EU hospitals under GDPR, and for any institution with strict IT security policies, this is a significant concern.

Pisum solves this: all AI processing happens locally on your CPU or GPU. The same accuracy, zero cloud dependency.
